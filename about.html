about me

Chloé Smits

contact

If someone had told me five years ago that I would be living in Sydney, working in tech, and learning how to code in my free time, I would have burst out laughing and thought they had mistaken me for someone else.
Originally from Belgium, I grew up in the picturesque town of Mechelen and worked as a media producer for most of my life. Although I had a strong passion for my work in this highly creative industry, it could also be emotionally taxing at times. That's why our move to Sydney presented an opportunity to take a break, reflect, and rediscover myself. Being open to new experiences, I eagerly jumped at the opportunity to explore the world of tech and was pleasantly surprised by how well it suited my personality. My drive to succeed, paired with my strong organizational skills, communication abilities, and attention to detail, have allowed me to thrive in this new field. With that said, I am thrilled to welcome you to my portfolio website, the final project for the responsive course at SheCodes!
